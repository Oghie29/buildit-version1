Welcome to Build-it, a DIY (do it yourself)community-driven social sharing decentralized platform born out of the need to support and reward DIYers and Hands-on projects with cryptocurrency. At Build-it, it is our utmost desire to empower people of all ages and culture to be in control of their money and establish and form their own wealth opportunities. The $buidl native tokens is our currency upon which users can trade back for real money value, it’s built on the Hive Blockchain (web3) with a smart algorithm that makes it safe to Invest in and beneficial by just holding it.
